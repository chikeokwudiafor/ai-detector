About AIthentic

AIthentic uses advanced detection techniques to verify AI-generated content with professional-grade accuracy.

Our Approach

AIthentic employs an ensemble-based detection system that combines multiple state-of-the-art AI models to provide reliable and accurate detection of AI-generated content. Our system analyzes images, text, and videos using weighted voting algorithms and confidence scoring to deliver trustworthy results.

We use a multi-model approach with fallback mechanisms to ensure high availability and robust performance across different types of content and generation methods.

Detection Technologies

Our detection capabilities are powered by the following advanced AI models and techniques:

Text Detection
RoBERTa OpenAI Detector
A fine-tuned RoBERTa model specifically designed to detect GPT-generated text with high accuracy.
Text Detection
ChatGPT Detector RoBERTa

Ensemble Method

Quality Assurance

Our system includes comprehensive logging, confidence scoring, and agreement metrics to ensure transparency and reliability. We continuously monitor model performance and update our detection capabilities to stay ahead of evolving AI generation techniques.

All predictions include detailed confidence scores and model agreement metrics, allowing users to understand the reliability of each detection result.
